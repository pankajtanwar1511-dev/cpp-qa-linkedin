HOW TO USE:
1. Run: python3 automation/qa_poster.py --post-question
   → Posts next [ ] question, marks as [Q]

2. Wait 1 hour (automated by pending_comments.json)

3. Run: python3 automation/qa_poster.py --post-answers
   → Posts pending answers, marks [Q] → [X]

OR use auto mode:
   python3 automation/qa_poster.py --auto
   → Automatically posts question OR answers based on state

========================================
FILES REQUIRED FOR EACH POST:
  Post N requires these 4 files in data/data/:
  - NN_content.txt          (Question caption for main post)
  - NN_question_image.png   (Code screenshot/diagram)
  - NN_answer.txt           (Answer text for comment)
  - NN_answer_image.png     (Full explanation diagram)

Example for Post 1:
  - 01_content.txt
  - 01_question_image.png
  - 01_answer.txt
  - 01_answer_image.png
